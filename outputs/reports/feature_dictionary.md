# Feature Dictionary

## customer_metrics
- `customer_id`: unique customer identifier.
- `segment`: customer segment from source master data.
- `region`: customer region from source master data.
- `acquisition_channel`: channel attributed at acquisition.
- `first_transaction_date`: earliest observed transaction date for the customer.
- `last_transaction_date`: latest observed transaction date for the customer.
- `lifetime_days`: `(last_transaction_date - first_transaction_date) + 1`; `0` when no transactions.
- `total_revenue`: sum of `revenue` across all customer transactions.
- `total_cost`: sum of `cost` across all customer transactions.
- `contribution_margin`: `total_revenue - total_cost`.
- `contribution_margin_pct`: `contribution_margin / total_revenue`; `0` when `total_revenue = 0`.
- `transaction_count`: number of transactions for the customer.
- `avg_revenue_per_transaction`: `total_revenue / transaction_count`; `0` when `transaction_count = 0`.
- `revenue_per_day`: `total_revenue / lifetime_days`; `0` when `lifetime_days = 0`.

## cohort_table
- `cohort_month`: customer signup month (first day of month).
- `activity_month`: transaction month (first day of month).
- `customers_active`: distinct customers from the cohort with at least one transaction in activity month.
- `cohort_revenue`: total revenue generated by active customers in that cohort/activity month pair.
- `average_revenue_per_active_customer`: `cohort_revenue / customers_active`.

## unit_economics
- `acquisition_channel`: acquisition channel.
- `customers_acquired`: number of customers acquired through the channel.
- `total_spend`: sum of marketing spend for the channel.
- `CAC`: `total_spend / customers_acquired`.
- `average_LTV`: average customer LTV by channel.
- `median_LTV`: median customer LTV by channel.
- `LTV_to_CAC`: `average_LTV / CAC`.
- `approximate_payback_period`: `CAC / avg_monthly_contribution_margin_per_customer` (months), null when denominator <= 0.
